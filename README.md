## 🖌 Overview  

Audio Hijack Professional Suite is a professional audio capture and routing platform that gives you complete control over every audio source on your system, recorded and processed through a flexible visual session editor.

Multiple simultaneous sessions let you handle complex recording scenarios — capturing a podcast interview, recording backing music, and monitoring your own microphone through processing — all running concurrently without conflict.

Timer-based and schedule-based recording automates capture of live streams, radio programs, or any recurring audio source, building a library of recordings without requiring manual intervention.

The block ecosystem continues to grow with new input types, processing options, and output formats that extend Audio Hijack Professional Suite's capabilities to match evolving production needs.

Audio Hijack Professional Suite is the audio capture foundation that podcasters, radio producers, musicians, and content creators rely on when they need to record any sound from any source with professional quality and complete reliability.
